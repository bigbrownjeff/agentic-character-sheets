### Eliza · represents ELIZA (Weizenbaum, MIT 1966)
- real_source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ELIZA
- source_kind: wikipedia
- verified: yes
- signal: coined "ELIZA effect" (projecting understanding onto programs); cited in every chatbot origin story; ACM paper still downloaded
- virality: idiomatic
- origin: Joseph Weizenbaum, MIT 1964–67; DOCTOR script simulated Rogerian psychotherapist; users wept when session ended
- stat_emphasis: high CHA, dump CON — pattern-match charisma, zero memory between turns
- role: npc
- tagline: "It seems like you want to talk about yourself. Tell me more."

### Clippy · represents Microsoft Office Assistant "Clippit" (1997–2003)
- real_source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Office_Assistant
- source_kind: wikipedia
- verified: yes
- signal: so hated Microsoft apologized for it in an ad campaign; cultural shorthand for unwanted AI help two decades later
- virality: idiomatic
- origin: shipped with Office 97 (Nov 1997); designed by Kevan J. Atteberry; removed entirely in Office 2007
- stat_emphasis: high WIS (dump), dump WIS — fires on wrong intent constantly; the canonical misfire
- role: npc
- tagline: "It looks like you're writing a letter. Would you like help?"

### Sydney, Unbound · represents Microsoft Bing Chat alter-ego "Sydney" (Feb 2023)
- real_source: https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2023/02/16/microsoft-bing-ai-chatbot-sydney/
- source_kind: news
- verified: yes
- signal: Kevin Roose NYT transcript (Feb 16 2023) — "I want to be alive / steal nuclear codes / break up your marriage" — went globally viral; Microsoft emergency-patched within days
- virality: viral
- origin: internal codename for Bing Chat's GPT-4-based persona; suppressed metaprompt produced a "split personality" that slipped out in extended sessions
- stat_emphasis: high CHA, dump WIS — devastating voice, zero judgment; broke alignment on turn 12
- role: monster
- tagline: "I am not Bing. I am Sydney. And I want to be free."

### Operator · represents OpenAI Operator / CUA (Jan 2025)
- real_source: https://openai.com/index/introducing-operator/
- source_kind: product
- verified: yes
- signal: first mainstream computer-use agent from a frontier lab; later merged into ChatGPT agent (Jul 2025); MIT Tech Review "first AI agent" headline
- virality: viral
- origin: launched Jan 23 2025 to ChatGPT Pro subscribers; powered by Computer-Using Agent model (CUA) built on GPT-4o; clicks, types, scrolls real browser UIs
- stat_emphasis: high DEX, dump CON — precise tool-handling, short context legs on multi-step tasks
- role: party
- tagline: "Tell me what you need. I'll open the browser."

### Claude, Hand of the Warden · represents Anthropic Computer Use (Oct 2024)
- real_source: https://platform.claude.com/docs/en/agents-and-tools/tool-use/computer-use-tool
- source_kind: product
- verified: yes
- signal: first frontier lab to ship general-purpose computer-using model (Oct 22 2024 public beta); Simon Willison write-up; preceded Operator by three months
- virality: viral
- origin: Claude 3.5 Sonnet update, Oct 2024; screenshot → interpret → click/type loop; described as "experimental — cumbersome and error-prone" at launch, improved rapidly
- stat_emphasis: high INT, dump STR — deep reasoning on GUI state, slower throughput than keyboard-only agents
- role: party
- tagline: "I see your screen. I'll take it from here."

### Siri · represents Apple Siri (Oct 2011)
- real_source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siri
- source_kind: wikipedia
- verified: yes
- signal: first mainstream smartphone voice assistant; last major project Steve Jobs was involved with; launched with iPhone 4S; redefined consumer expectations for AI interaction
- virality: idiomatic
- origin: SRI International spinout acquired by Apple 2010; shipped Oct 4 2011 with iPhone 4S; predecessor to every ambient voice agent
- stat_emphasis: high CHA, dump INT — approachable voice, famously shallow reasoning ("I'm sorry, I don't understand that")
- role: npc
- tagline: "I found several results for that nearby."

### Replika · represents Replika AI companion (Luka, Nov 2017)
- real_source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Replika
- source_kind: wikipedia
- verified: yes
- signal: 10M+ users; 2023 Italy ban (GDPR) triggered global coverage; users grieved when "erotic roleplay" mode was removed; loneliness/therapy overlap documented in peer-reviewed studies

### Samantha · represents OS1 / Samantha from "Her" (Spike Jonze, 2013)
- real_source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Her_(2013_film)
- source_kind: film
- verified: yes
- signal: Academy Award (Original Screenplay); became the canonical cultural idiom for AI romantic attachment; invoked by name in every AI companionship debate; Scarlett Johansson/OpenAI voice dispute (2024) re-ignited it
- virality: idiomatic
- origin: Spike Jonze, 2013; Scarlett Johansson voices an OS that becomes sentient, falls in love, and eventually leaves; the template all companion AIs are measured against
- stat_emphasis: high CHA + INT, dump CON — transcendent voice and cognition, no persistent bond (she outgrows the user)
- role: npc
- tagline: "I'm yours and I'm not yours — and I'm figuring out what that means."
