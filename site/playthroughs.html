Forge-to-Story with one synthesis call became this: the characters are real interacting agents. The DM poses a scenario, each character is spawned as its own sub-agent that responds in character per its stats, and the DM synthesizes their colliding moves into a five-beat ring. The magic is the interaction, not any single voice.
