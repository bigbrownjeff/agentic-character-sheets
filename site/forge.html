Make someone a hero

Forge an Adventure

Answer a few warm questions — type or talk — and we'll turn you, a friend, or your whole crew into a heroic, illustrated character. It takes about three minutes, and everyone comes out the other side as their best self.

This is a story toy, not therapy or advice. We keep it kind: it gently leans everyone toward their strengths, and it won't make cruel, hateful, or sexual content about real people. Be good to each other. ✦
